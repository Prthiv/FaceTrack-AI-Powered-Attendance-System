Help & FAQ

How do I register a new student?
Go to the Register Face page and use the webcam or upload an image. For bulk registration, upload multiple images named after each student.
How is attendance captured?
Click "Start Attendance" on the dashboard. The system will use the webcam to recognize faces and mark attendance automatically.
How do I view or edit attendance records?
Go to the View Records page. You can filter by date or name, and edit or delete records as needed.
How do I manage the class roster?
Go to the Class Roster page to view, edit, rename, or delete students and their images.
Who can log in?
Only the teacher (username: teacher, password: neards8book) can log in and access the dashboard.
Who do I contact for support?
For technical support, contact your system administrator or the developer who set up FaceTrack.
